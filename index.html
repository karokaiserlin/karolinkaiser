Hello

Hi! I’m Karolin, and I will be joining the Linguistics Department at MIT in September. I did my undergrad at Humboldt University in Berlin.

At the most general level, I am interested in the core meaning components expressed by natural language. My research focuses on understanding how functional morphemes--whose meanings we usually assume to be tightly restricted--defy the traditional semantics associated with them when studied outside their expected (morpho)syntactic contexts. This comprises two interrelated questions that form the foundation of my broader research goals. First, to what extent can the morphological patterns typically linked tied to certain types of ‘meaning blocks’ reveal deep insights about these ties or these blocks? And second, how do cross-linguistic variations in these patters challenge or support our existing theoretical frameworks?
